# Automate Your WalkMe Digital Adoption Platform with n8n

We're excited to announce the release of our latest community node: n8n-nodes-walkme! Velocity BPA has developed this integration to help organizations streamline their digital adoption workflows and eliminate manual tasks around user provisioning, analytics, and platform management.

## The Challenge

WalkMe is a powerful digital adoption platform that helps organizations guide users through complex software experiences. However, managing WalkMe at scale often involves repetitive tasks: provisioning users across systems, extracting analytics data for reporting, synchronizing user information, and coordinating digital adoption campaigns with other business processes. These manual workflows consume valuable time and can lead to inconsistencies.

## The Solution

The n8n-nodes-walkme integration brings WalkMe's capabilities directly into your n8n automation workflows. Now you can connect WalkMe with hundreds of other applications and services, creating powerful automation scenarios that were previously impossible or required custom development.

## Key Features

- **User Provisioning Automation**: Automatically create, update, or remove WalkMe users based on triggers from your HR systems, identity providers, or other sources
- **Analytics Integration**: Extract WalkMe analytics data and push it to your data warehouse, BI tools, or reporting dashboards
- **Digital Adoption Workflows**: Trigger WalkMe experiences based on events in other systems, creating contextual user guidance
- **Multi-System Synchronization**: Keep user data consistent across WalkMe and your other business applications

## Getting Started

Installing the WalkMe node is simple. In your n8n instance, run:


npm install n8n-nodes-walkme


After installation, restart n8n and you'll find the WalkMe node available in your node palette.
